MAPPING AND RANDOM


7 November 2019 | Javascript , p5.js

Map:


This function re-maps a number from one range to another.It takes five parameters.

map( variable , variable's set range(min) , variable's set range(max) , new range(min) , new range(max))

In this case, we are going to map one range which is the position of mouse along x-axis starting from 0 in the left most side, ending at the width of canvas on right most side , to a new range of minimum 10 and maximum 200.

let h = map(mouseX,0,width,10, 200)

Other examples:

let k = map(mouseY,0,height,40,100)

let e = map(sin(angle),-1,1,10,20)



Random:


random(min. value , max. value)
